Identity primary layer

Each system, operator or artificial agent is associated with a verifiable identity primary record enabling direct attribution of operational actions.

Ex-ante authorization

Operational actions can be linked to a preventive verification stage ensuring coherence, legitimacy and continuity before execution.

Continuous audit chain

Each operation is inserted into a continuous temporal sequence ensuring traceability and operational continuity across infrastructures.

Temporal opponibility

All actions remain verifiable and attributable over time through a non-retroactive audit structure ensuring persistent responsibility.

Distributed European infrastructure

The IPR Control Stack operates as a distributed and interoperable layer compatible with existing European regulatory and technological frameworks.

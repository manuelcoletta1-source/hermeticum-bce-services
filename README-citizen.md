# HERMETICUM B.C.E. — CITIZEN
**Your European Digital Identity, Protected Over Time**

Hermeticum B.C.E. is a **European digital platform** that helps you
**protect your digital identity and important documents over time**.

It ensures that your identity remains **verifiable, intact and valid**,
even if you change country, job, or legal situation.

---

## What Problem Does It Solve?

Digital documents can be:
- lost
- altered
- disputed
- rejected after time passes

Hermeticum B.C.E. creates a **verifiable proof** that:
- you are who you claim to be
- your data has not been modified
- the proof remains valid over time

---

## What You Get as a Citizen

With Hermeticum B.C.E. you can:
- create a **verifiable digital identity**
- protect documents and credentials
- prove authenticity when needed
- maintain continuity across EU countries

The platform works **independently of any single provider or country**.

---

- cryptography

- legal procedures

1. Your identity information is verified  
2. A digital proof is created

- cannot be altered
- can be independently verified
- remains valid in the future

EU Pilot Framework for human attribution, decision accountability and long-term digital evidence in high-risk systems.

What this is

The BioCyber Identity Card is not a new civil identity and does not replace national documents. It is a technical accountability layer designed to ensure that no critical digital decision can occur without a real, accountable human behind it.

This repository documents the minimal, fail-closed framework required to make responsibility, verification and long-term proof non-optional in digital systems.

The problem

  • Critical decisions executed without human attribution
  • Automation without ex-ante verification
  • Digital evidence that decays or becomes legally unusable over time

These structural failures produce legal uncertainty, operational incidents and systemic responsibility gaps.

The solution

The BioCyber Identity Card defines a technical chain of accountability that guarantees:

  • explicit attribution to a real human being
  • mandatory validation before execution
  • verifiable, long-term digital evidence

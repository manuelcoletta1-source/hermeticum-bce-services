EU Pilot — Technical Specification

What this is

The BioCyber Identity Card is not a new civil identity and does not replace national documents. It is a technical accountability layer designed to ensure that no critical digital decision can occur without a real, accountable human behind it.

The problem

  • critical decisions executed without human attribution
  • automation without mandatory ex-ante validation
  • digital evidence that decays or becomes legally unusable

These failures create legal uncertainty, operational incidents and systemic responsibility gaps.

The solution

The BioCyber Identity Card defines a minimal, fail-closed chain that guarantees:

  • explicit attribution to a real human being
  • mandatory validation before execution
  • verifiable, long-term digital evidence

Core components

  • Human Root Reference (HRR) — technical human attribution
  • Decision Gate Validator (DGV) — fail-closed validation
  • Certified Time & Trace — metrologically valid timestamps
  • Long-Term Evidence Vault — proof preservation ≥ 20 years

Scope

Designed for high-risk domains such as:

  • AI systems under the EU AI Act
  • critical healthcare
  • C2 / C4ISR environments
  • automated public procurement
